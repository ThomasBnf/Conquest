The Conquest Discord integration enables you to access and manage community data from your Discord server. Gain insights into member activities, threads, messages, replies, reactions history, all in one place.



## Data collection

### Members

The integration collects information about:
- **Full Name**
- **Username**
- **Avatar** if provided
- **Join Date**
- **Roles**

We do not collect:
- **Email**


### Conversations

- **Active Threads** from public channels
- **Archived Threads** from public channels
- **Messages** from public channels
- **Replies** from public channels
- **Reactions** from public channels
- **Files** from public channels

## Activity Types

When you connect your Discord server, activity types are automatically generated and configured.

<Tip>
  Activity weights can be customized to fit your community's scoring system.
</Tip>

- **Invitation** - 7 weight
- **Write a post** - 6 weight
- **Reply to post** - 5 weight
- **Join Discord community** - 4 weight
- **Add reaction** - 0 weight

## Real-Time Updates

Once connected, Conquest syncs the following events in real-time:
- **Members joining the workspace**
- **Threads, messages, replies, reactions, and files shared in public channels**
- **Creation, updates, and deletion of public channels**

## Setup guide

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect Your Workspace">
    1. Navigate to **Settings** > **Integrations**.
    2. Select **Discord**.
    3. Click **"Enable"** to start the connection process.

    <Frame>
      ![Discord Connection](/images/discord-enable.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Authorize Access">
    Complete the OAuth process on Discord.
    <Tip>
      You must be an admin of the Discord server.
    </Tip>

    <Frame>
      ![Discord OAuth](/images/discord-oauth.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select Channels">
    Once authorized, you'll be redirected to Conquest to select public channels for data collection.

    1. Select the channels you want to collect data from
    2. Click **"Let's Start"**


    ### Missing channels? 
      Verify that the **@everyone** role has the required permissions: **"View Channel"** and **"Read Message History"**. 
      If not, and you are not able to, add the **"Conquest"** role to the channel with these permissions enabled.


    <Frame>
      ![Discord view channel](/images/discord-view-channel.png)
    </Frame>
    <br/>
     <Frame>
      ![Discord read message history](/images/discord-read-message-history.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Data Collection">
    Conquest will begin importing historical data.
    <Frame>
      ![Discord Collect](/images/discord-collect.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  The data collection duration depends on your community size and can take from a few minutes to several hours to complete.
</Warning>
