The Conquest Discord integration enables you to access and manage community data from your Discord server. Gain insights into member activities, threads, messages, replies, reactions history, all in one place.

<Note>
  We only collect data from the last 365 days.
</Note>

## Data collection

The integration collects information about:

### Members

* **Full Name**
* **Username**
* **Avatar** if provided
* **Join server date**
* **Roles**

We do not collect:

* **Email**
* **Bio**

### Conversations

* **Active Threads** from public channels
* **Archived Threads** from public channels
* **Messages** from public channels
* **Replies** from public channels
* **Reactions** from public channels


## Activity Types

When you connect your Discord server, activity types are automatically generated and integrated.

<Tip>
  Activity points can be customized to fit your community's scoring system.
</Tip>

* **Create a thread**
* **Reply to thread**
* **Write a message**
* **Reply to message**
* **Add reaction**

## Real-Time Updates

Once connected, Conquest syncs the following events in real-time:

* **Members joining the server**
* **Members updates**
* **Members removed from the server**
* **Threads, messages, replies, reactions in public channels**
* **Creation, updates, and deletion of public channels**
* **Creation, updates, and deletion of roles**


## Setup guide

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect Your Workspace">
    1. Navigate to **Settings** > **Integrations**.

    2. Select **Discord**.

    3. Click **"Enable"** to start the connection process.

    <Frame>
      ![Discord Connection](/images/discord/enable.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Authorize Access">
    Complete the OAuth process on Discord.

    <Tip>
      You must be an admin of the Discord server.
    </Tip>

    <Frame>
      ![Discord OAuth](/images/discord/oauth.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Channels">
    Once authorized, you'll be redirected to Conquest to select public channels for data collection.

    1. Select the channels you want to collect data from

     <Frame>
      ![Discord Collect](/images/discord/channels.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure Activity Types">
    Conquest automatically generates activity types based on the data collected.
    You can add conditions on specific channels to fit your community's scoring system.

    <Frame>
      ![Discord activity types](/images/discord/activity-types.png)
    </Frame>

    <br />
    
    <Frame>
      ![Discord activity types](/images/discord/condition.png)
    </Frame>
   
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  The data collection duration depends on your community size and can take from a few minutes to several hours to complete.
</Warning>
