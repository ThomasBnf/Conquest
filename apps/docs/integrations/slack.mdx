The Conquest Slack integration connects with your Slack workspace, enabling comprehensive management of your community data. Access all workspace members, public channels, message history, replies, files and reactions.

## Features

<Note>If you are in free plan, you will only be able to collect data from the last 90 days.</Note>

### Members
What we collect:
- **Full Name**
- **Email**
- **Job Title** if provided
- **Phone** if provided
- **Avatar**
- **Locale**

What we don't collect (not available in Slack API):
- **Joined Date**

### Conversations
- **Messages** from public channels
- **Replies** from public channels
- **Reactions** from public channels
- **Files** from public channels

### Real-Time Updates
Once we are connected to your workspace, we will get real-time updates on:  
- **Members joining the workspace**
- **Messages, replies and reactions**
- **Files shared to public channels**
- **Public channel creation, updates and deletion**
- **App uninstallation**

## Merging Members

<Tip>
  When new Slack activity occurs, we automatically:
  1. Check if the member exists in your Conquest workspace
  2. Create a new member profile if they don't exist
  3. Merge with an existing profile if we find a matching email
</Tip>


## Connecting to Slack

<Steps>
  <Step title="Workspace Connection">
    1. Go to the **Integrations** section in settings page
    2. Select **Slack**
    3. Click "Enable" to begin the connection process

    <Frame>
    ![Slack Connection](/images/slack-enable.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="OAuth">
    After clicking "Enable", you will be redirected to Slack to complete the OAuth process.
    <Tip>
     You have to be an Admin of the Slack workspace to enable the integration.
    </Tip>

    <Frame>
    ![Slack OAuth](/images/slack-oauth.png)
    </Frame>
    
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Channels selection">
    One you have allowed Conquest integration, you will be redirected back to Conquest and see the list of public channels in your workspace.
    <Tip>
     If you don't see any channels, channels may be private.
    </Tip>
     <Frame>
    ![Slack Channels](/images/slack-channels.png)
    </Frame>
    1. Select the channels you want to collect data from
    2. Click "Let's Start"
  </Step>
  <Step title="Collecting">
   Conquest will start collecting data from the selected channels in the last 365 days maximum.
   <Note>
    If you have a free plan, you will only be able to collect data from the last 90 days.
   </Note>
    <Frame>
    ![Slack Collecting](/images/slack-collecting.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

  <Warning>
    The data collection duration depends on your community size and can take from a few minutes to several hours to complete.
  </Warning>
