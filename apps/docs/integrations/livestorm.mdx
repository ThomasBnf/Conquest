The Conquest Livestorm integration enables you to connect your Livestorm workspace and access all your webinar sessions and participant data.
Track registrations, attendance, co-hosting information from your webinars in one centralized place.

## Data collection

The integration collects information about:

### Members

* **Full Name**

* **Email**

* **Avatar** if provided

* **Locale**

### Events

* **Sessions details**

* **Registrants**

* **Co-hosts**

* **Attendees**

## Activity Types

When you connect your Livestorm workspace, activity types are automatically generated and integrated.

<Tip>
  Activity weights can be customized to fit your community's scoring system.
</Tip>

* **Co-host a webinar** - 10 weight

* **Attend a webinar** - 8 weight

* **Register for a webinar** - 5 weight

## Real-Time Updates

Once connected, Conquest syncs the following events in real-time:

* **Member registration**

* **Member attendance**

* **Member co-hosting**

## Merging Members

<Tip>
  When new Livestorm activity occurs, we automatically:

  1. Check if the member exists in your Conquest workspace

  2. Create a new member if  doesn't exist

  3. Merge with an existing member if we find a matching email
</Tip>

## Setup guide

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect Your Workspace">
    1. Navigate to **Settings** > **Integrations**.

    2. Select **Livestorm**.

    3. Click **"Enable"** to start the connection process.

    <Frame>
      ![Livestorm Connection](/images/livestorm-enable.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Authorize Access">
    Complete the OAuth process on Discord.

    <Tip>
      You must be an admin of the Discord server.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Filter Events">
    Once authorized, you'll be redirected to Conquest.

    <Tip>
      You can filter the events you want to collect by entering keyword to exclude marketing or other non-community webinars.
    </Tip>

    <Frame>
      ![Livestorm filter events](/images/livestorm-filter-events.png)
    </Frame>

    Then, click **"Let's Start"** to begin events collection
  </Step>

  <Step title="Data Collection">
    Conquest will begin importing historical data.

    {/* <Frame>
          ![Discord Collect](/images/discord-collect.png)
        </Frame> */}
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  The data collection duration depends on your number of events and can take from a few minutes to several hours to complete.
</Warning>
